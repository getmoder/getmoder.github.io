What is Early Birds Private Sales Event?

Early Birds Private Sales Event is an exclusive “early bird” opportunity for people to purchase tokens/coins before they go public about the ITO. 


The pre-sale usually comes with two main advantages over the public sale:

1) Price. Token prices are much cheaper than in the public sale. 

2) Bonus. Early buyers could benefit from additional free bonus during the pre-sale phase which is often not the case when the token goes to the crowd sale.


In our case, it is open only to accredited investors.  Not everyone gets a chance at the Early Birds offerings, it’s reserved for special groups of people such as such as strategic buyers, family members, close friends, business associates and other members in close circles.

How does Loan works?
1. Loan Creation - A borrower sends collateral to the Lender collateral wallet and funds/tokens are transferred to the borrower’s wallet account. Collateral remains the property of the borrower and any price appreciation or depreciation belongs to the borrower.

2. Loan Repayment - A borrower makes timely, periodic payments to the lender.

3. Loan Completion - Upon repayment of the loan, the borrower’s collateral is returned.

The platform will not provide a pre-set interest rate. It will be fully market-dependent. The lender will set the interest rate in his/her loan application, and the borrower will state the desired interest rate for getting a loan. The system will automatically bring the matching applications together.

Missed payment
If a borrower misses a payment, SLBN will liquidate a portion of the total collateral to cover both the missed payment and any associated fees. If a borrower continues to miss payments, the collateral will continue to be liquidated to cover monthly payments of principal, interest and fees until the loan-to-value ratio exceeds a predetermined threshold. Once the default reaches the triggering threshold described above, a balance of the collateral is liquidated to cover the missed payment and to calibrate the over-collateralization. This process continues until the loan is fully retired or matures. Lenders may elect to receive their funds in either the currency of the original loan or the equivalent value in blockchain assets. After the principal of the loan and accrued fees are paid out of the collateral, any remaining assets are returned to the borrower.
